About Creative Digitalist

“If there’s a book you want to read, but it hasn’t been written yet, then you must write it.” – Toni Morrison

These words resonate far beyond writing a book. They speak to creation itself: that which hasn’t yet been made but is deeply desired must be created by those who desire it.

Such were the circumstances in which Creative Digitalist was born—a professional identity not yet widely defined or recognized. After a lifetime of varied interests and interdisciplinary curiosities, I took a direction that was at once unexpected and natural: forging a career path that doesn’t quite exist… yet.

With every project and experience, the book is being written, the story is being shaped, and Creative Digitalist is steadily finding its place.

Explore my journey, projects, and experiments as this story continues to unfold.
